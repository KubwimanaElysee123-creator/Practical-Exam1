Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How far in advance should I book a tour?

A: We recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance for popular tours like gorilla trekking. However, we'll do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Q: What's your cancellation policy?

A: Cancellations made 14 days before the tour date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 14 days may have fees depending on circumstances.

Q: Do you offer group discounts?

A: Yes! Groups of 8 or more qualify for special discounts. Contact us for a custom group quote.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money. A 30% deposit secures your booking with the balance due 2 weeks before the tour.

Q: Is travel insurance included?

A: Yes, basic travel insurance is included. We recommend purchasing additional comprehensive travel insurance through your provider.
