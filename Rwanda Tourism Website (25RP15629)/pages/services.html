What We Offer

We provide comprehensive tourism services tailored to create the perfect Rwanda experience for every traveler.

🦍 Gorilla Trekking Tours

Experience the world's most iconic wildlife encounter in Volcanoes National Park. Our expert guides lead you on a guided trek through misty forests to observe these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

Duration: 1-3 days | Price: $1,500 - $3,000

🦁 Safari Tours

Explore Akagera National Park and find lions, elephants, buffalo, and a variety of antelope species. Our safari packages include game drives, walking safaris, and birdwatching expeditions.

Duration: 2-4 days | Price: $800 - $1,800

💧 Lake & Water Tours

Relax on the shores of Lake Kivu or Lake Bunyonyi. Enjoy boat trips, fishing, island hopping, swimming, and lakeside picnics with stunning scenic views.

Duration: 1-2 days | Price: $300 - $800

🌲 Forest & Nature Hikes

Trek through the lush Nyungwe Forest to spot golden monkeys and over 300 bird species. We also offer waterfall visits and forest canopy walks.

Duration: 1-2 days | Price: $400 - $1,000

🎭 Cultural & Community Tours

Immerse yourself in Rwandan culture by visiting traditional villages, learning crafts, participating in cooking classes, and connecting with local communities.

Duration: 1 day | Price: $150 - $400

🏨 Accommodation & Hospitality

We arrange luxury hotels, eco-lodges, budget guesthouses, and camping facilities across all regions of Rwanda to suit your preferences and budget.

Options: Luxury to Budget | Price: $50 - $500/night

🚗 Private Tours & Transportation

Enjoy comfortable private vehicle tours with experienced drivers and guides. We handle all logistics so you can relax and enjoy your journey.

Duration: Flexible | Price: From $100/day

📸 Photography & Art Tours

Perfect for photographers and artists! Our specialized tours focus on capturing stunning landscapes, wildlife, and cultural moments with expert guidance.

Duration: 2-7 days | Price: $1,200 - $2,500
