Rwanda Tourism Report

This report highlights the latest findings and observations about tourism in Rwanda. It includes visitor trends, destination performance, and recommendations for travelers seeking new experiences.

Key Insights

Rwanda continues to attract visitors from around the world due to its unique wildlife, cultural heritage, and commitment to sustainability. The country's tourism industry is evolving with an emphasis on conservation and community-based travel.

Visitor Feedback

Many travelers praise Rwanda for its warm hospitality, safe environment, and unforgettable gorilla trekking adventures. The growth of eco-friendly lodges and guided tours has made the destination even more appealing.

Recommendations

For those planning a trip, we recommend allowing at least one week to explore Kigali, Nyungwe Forest, Lake Kivu, and Volcanoes National Park. Be sure to book gorilla permits early and consider combining wildlife excursions with cultural experiences.
