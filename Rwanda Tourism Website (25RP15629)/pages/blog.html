Travel Tips & Updates

✈️ Visa Information

Most visitors can obtain a Rwanda visa on arrival or apply online. Processing is quick and straightforward. Check current requirements before traveling.

📅 Best Time to Visit

Rwanda has two rainy seasons. For the best experience, visit during June-September or December-January. Clear skies and excellent wildlife viewing await.

💊 Health Precautions

Consult your doctor about vaccinations like yellow fever and malaria prophylaxis. Tap water in hotels is safe. Travel insurance is highly recommended.

💰 Currency & Money

The Rwandan Franc (RWF) is the local currency. ATMs are available in Kigali and major towns. USD is accepted in tourist areas. Credit cards work at most hotels.

📱 Communication

Excellent mobile networks and WiFi in hotels. Get a local SIM card for internet access during your stay. Roaming charges apply if using your home provider.

🗣️ Language

Kinyarwanda is the official language, but English and French are widely spoken in tourist areas. Learning a few phrases goes a long way in making local connections.
